01

Prioritize Self-Care

Take time for yourself each day to relax and recharge.
Engage in activities you enjoy, whether it's reading, exercising, meditating, or spending time outdoors.
02

Practice Mindfulness

Mindfulness techniques, such as meditation and deep breathing exercises, can
help reduce stress and promote a sense of calm and clarity.
03

Seek Help When Needed

Don't hesitate to reach out for support if you're struggling. Talk to a
trusted friend,family member, or mental health professional if you're experiencing persistent
distress or symptoms of mental illness.
