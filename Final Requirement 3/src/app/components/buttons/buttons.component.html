Mental health problems are more common in today's stressful and fast-paced environment, but they are still usually stereotyped and misunderstood. In order to address this serious problem, our advocacy is to increase understanding, provide support, and increase awareness among those who are impacted by mental health issues. We work together and take proactive steps to support, reduce ignorance, and build a society that is more accepting and caring.
